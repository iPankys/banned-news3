  <span>
   不干净
  </span>
  <span>
   ”
  </span>

  <span>
   历史不干净
  </span>
  <span>
   ”
  </span>

  </span>
 </p>
 <p>
  <span>

  <span>

  </span>
  <span>

  <span>

  <span>

  </span>
  <span>
   青出于蓝胜于蓝
  </span>
  <span>
   ”
  </span>
  <span>

  </span>
  <span>

  </span>
  <span>
   江青
  </span>
  <span>
   ”

 </p>
 <p>
  <span>

  </span>
  <span>

  </span>
  <span>
   从影
  </span>
  <span>

  </span>
  <span>

  </span>
  <span>

  </span>
  <span>

  </span>

 <p>
  <span>

  </span>
 </p>
 <p>

  </span>
  <span>

  </span>
  <span>

  </span>
  <span>
   ”
  </span>

  </span>
  <span>

  </span>

  </span>
  <span>
   ”
  </span>
  <span>

    <span>
    </span>
   </a>
  </span>
  <span>
   的好官
  </span>
  <span>
   ”
  </span>
  <span>

  </span>
  <span>
   “
  </span>
  <span>
   文革

  <span>
