   CDC

   强生公司
  </ok>
  疫苗造成罕见
  <ok href="https://www.ntdtv.com/gb/血栓.htm">
   血栓
  </ok>

  <ok href="https://www.ntdtv.com/gb/mrna疫苗.htm">
   mRNA疫苗
  </ok>

  <ok href="https://www.ntdtv.com/gb/血栓.htm">
